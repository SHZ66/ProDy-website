PRS for Perturbation Response Scanning

PRS calculates the effectiveness and sensitivity of residues in propagation of allosteric signals through a protein.

Features

PRS uses as an input the known structure modeled as an elastic network (ANM). It may be used to evaluate a map of how perturbations each residue i affect the dynamics of each other residue j, as well as average effectiveness and sensitivity profiles for individual residues. The output can be used for identification of:

  1. effector residues that propagate signals into a protein in response to external perturbations
  2. sensor residues that sense allosteric signals coming from other residues and respond with altered dynamics
  3. important connections for signalling within a protein
