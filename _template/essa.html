Essential Site Scanning Analysis (ESSA)

ESSA identifies sites that modify the global/functional dynamics of a protein by perturbing each residue via crowding its local environmet in the elastic netwrok. Essentiality of a residue in response to a perturbation is measured ny the mean shift in the frequency of the softest modes.

API Features

You can use ESSA to

  • Detection of essential residues that are located at ligand binding, e.g. allosteric or orthosteric, sites or mechanical hinges
  • Generation of ESSA profile, namely the z-score of mean frequency shift for each resiude
  • Prediction of allosteric pockets for apo and holo structures via integration with Fpocket algorithm
