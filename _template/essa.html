Essential Site Scanning Analysis

ESSA calculates the essentiality of residues within a protein in terms of their ability to modulate its dynamics upon perturbation.

API Features

You can use ESSA to

  • create PDB ensembles of protein families from database searches
  • evaluate signature dynamics profiles and cross-correlations
  • calculate shared global mode profiles for assessing conserved motions among family members
  • assess departures from shared mode profiles and other protein-specific features
  • examine the spectral overlaps between family members
  • generate sequence-, structure- and dynamics-based trees for analyzing their evolution

Reference
Burak T Kaynak, Ivet Bahar, Pemra Doruker Essential site scanning analysis: A new approach for detecting sites that modulate the dispersion of protein global motions. Comput Struct Biotechnol J 2020 18:1577-1586
